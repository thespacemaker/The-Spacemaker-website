The AST MakerSpace

The AST MakerSpace was founded in 2016 at the American School of Tangier. It is part of a school-wide initiative to boost tech education in Tangier and the broader community. From 3D printing to woodworking to digital design, the AST MakerSpace uses the most modern tool-set to teach students how to build in the 21st century.
